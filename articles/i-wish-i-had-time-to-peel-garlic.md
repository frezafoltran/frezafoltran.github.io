date: 2026-02-01

As do Paul McCartney's songs, garlic sticks all over as I peel it.

I finish one clove, and the next one takes even longer.

The next one gets lost in its siblings dry peels.

In what feels like forever, I finish peeling all cloves.

I had so much time to catch up with my thoughts, I think.
