Being useful feels good, until I feel pressured to be useful.

Some days, I want to give space for my mind and body to remain calmly bored.

At times they feel the need to compensate for each other.

> > As my body rests, my mind races and vice-versa.

But today, **so that tomorrow I can race again**,

> > I wanna be in my birthday suit and rest in my crib.
